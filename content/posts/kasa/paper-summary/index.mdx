## Description

This paper focuses on using Natural Language Processing and transformer models to predict mental health and stress.
It looks at data from social media, such as Reddit, to find patterns in how people talk about their mental health.
The study compares different methods like TF-IDF, n-grams, BERT, RoBERTa, and ALBERT.
It found that RoBERTa works the best for predicting mental health conditions.

## Motivation

I chose this paper to learn how machine learning can be used to analyze detect mental health issues early, which is a growing societal concern.
I find it inspiring to see how data science can contribute to improving mental health care and using technology for social good.
