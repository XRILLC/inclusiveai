## Description

This paper is about a chatbot that was developed to assist Chinese students with their English language learning. Powered by AI, it gives adaptive feedback in engaging conversations to improve the student's listening and speaking skills. The conclusion is that conversational interfaces, such as EnglishBot, can benefit language learners' oral language skills.

## Motivation

I selected this paper because it heavily aligns with my interests in using technology to assist with language learning.
