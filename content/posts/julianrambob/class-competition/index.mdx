## Planned Approach
My plan is to train a model from sklearn (currently working with RandomForestClassifier) on data adjusted to refine feature weights. By using sentence-transformers to determine the similarity of text pairs, I can determine which data have similar texts by different authors and dissimilar texts by the same author. Training a model on these text pairs hopefully eliminates noise and improves performance on general data.
