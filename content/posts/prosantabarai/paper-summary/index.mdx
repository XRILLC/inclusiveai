## Description

This paper explores a method for semi-supervised learning called Unsupervised Data Augmentation (UDA), which leverages advanced data augmentation techniques to enhance model consistency on unlabeled data. By applying UDA, the authors achieved impressive results in both language and vision tasks, even with limited labeled data.

## Motivation

Data scarcity is a significant challenge for training large language models (LLMs), especially in specialized fields like healthcare. Since my research interest lies in healthcare LLMs, understanding advanced data augmentation techniques for semi-supervised learning is highly relevant to developing effective models in data-constrained domains.
