## Description

_This paper describes a novel approach to false information detection, utilizing Gated Recurrent Unit (GRU) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models with proposed modifications such as "adaptive learning rates" and a "hybrid attention mechanism". The research was conducted on a data set of varied digital content and the results suggested this novel approach outpreforms existing models while also reducing computational overhead._

## Motivation

_After reading a few research papers that were released in the last few years, I was struck by how often the papers were missing citations, cited papers that upon further review didn't state what the author stated they did, or included references to companies that were unsearchable through Google. It was then that I decided to explore the topics of false information and academic plagiarism. A major concern of mine when it comes to the usage of AI technology is the ease at which unknowingly and knowingly one can plagiarize work and how rapidly it (AI models) can disseminate false information._
