## Description

This study tracks the performance of several leading LLMs and their ability to retrieve relevant information within increasing dataset sizes. The experiments are structured to offer model-agnostic comparisons, including an analysis of effective context limits, tokenization differences, and the impact of increased context length on retrieval accuracy.

## Motivation

_Why did you select this paper?_
The paper initially caught my attention with its compelling title, which stood out from other other research papers. Its focus on evaluating the accuracy of LLMs in contextual data understanding resonated with the goals of my own paper, making it both informative and inspiring. Additionally, the paper struck a balance between depth and accessibility, providing an insightful way to deepen my understanding of LLMs within the scope of my current experience.
