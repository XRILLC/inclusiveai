### Project Goals
The goal of this project is to identify the best multimodal LLM for classifying image and (multi-lingual) text based data from Instagram. The data was collected using Selenium and Beautiful soup for my dissertation project. The content of the data is of a Mexican social movement and is therefore multi-lingual as a small portion of the posts are in English, the rest in Spanish.
- A secondary goal would be to turn the outputs of the classification into a graph database for further analysis. I think the first goal is a sufficient enough project to pursue in two weeks.

### Project Outline
- Identify a multimodal LMM that works with my data. i.e. GPT-4o, Phi3, LLama, etc.
- Clean and process collected Instagram data. Create subset for testing on the model
  - Mostly unsupervised data unless we finess it into hashtags or general themes but I'd like to not do that.
- Implement fine tuning: Here, potentially work with few-shot learning, transfer learning could be helpful. Even some sort of domain specific fine tuning could work especially dealing with social movements.
  - tokenize text data and segment image data
  - initialzie the model
  - evaluate
  - train the arguments, ie activation functions, validation, gradient/loss function, etc
  - look at batch sizes, epochs etc
- Evaluate overall and document your process.
- Set-up data for database creation.
